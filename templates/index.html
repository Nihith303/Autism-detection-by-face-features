Classification and Detection of Autism Spectrum Disorder Based on Deep Learning Algorithms.

Understanding Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)

Autism Spectrum Disorder is a developmental condition that affects communication, social interaction, and behavior. Early detection is crucial as it allows for timely intervention and support, significantly improving long-term outcomes.

How Our Detection Works

Our advanced deep learning model analyzes facial features and expressions that research has shown to be potential indicators of ASD, including:

  • Facial symmetry and proportions
  • Facial expression characteristics
  • Distinct facial landmarks and their relationships

Why Early Detection Matters

Early identification of ASD can lead to:

  • Earlier access to intervention services
  • Improved social and communication skills
  • Better academic and life outcomes
  • Targeted support for families and caregivers

Note: This tool is not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis. It is designed to assist in early screening and should be used in conjunction with professional evaluation.
